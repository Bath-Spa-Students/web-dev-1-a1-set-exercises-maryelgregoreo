Plot:

As Gotham City struggles under the grip of a new and violent vigilante known as the Red Hood, Batman finds himself in a relentless pursuit to uncover the truth behind this mysterious figure's identity and motives. The Red Hood's ruthless methods quickly escalate tensions within the criminal underworld, prompting Batman, Nightwing, and Alfred to investigate.

As they delve deeper into the Red Hood's past, they are forced to confront their own demons and revisit painful memories. Meanwhile, the Red Hood's actions draw the attention of both allies and adversaries, sparking conflicts that test the limits of morality and justice in Gotham City.

With each revelation, the stakes heighten, leading to a climactic showdown that forces Batman to confront the very essence of his crusade against crime. As secrets unravel and alliances are tested, the true nature of the Red Hood's vendetta becomes clearer, culminating in a confrontation that will forever alter the landscape of Gotham and its Dark Knight. Throughout the film, themes of redemption, revenge, and the blurred lines between heroism and vigilantism intertwine, creating a gripping narrative that captivates both heroes and audiences alike.
