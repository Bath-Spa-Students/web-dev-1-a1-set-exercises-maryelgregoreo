"Batman: Under the Red Hood" is an animated superhero movie based on the DC Comics storyline written by Judd Winick and produced by Warner Bros. Animation. Released on July 27, 2010, it is a part of the DC Universe Animated Original Movies series directed by Brandon Vietti. It features the voice cast of Bruce Greenwood as Batman, Jensen Ackles as the Red Hood, Neil Patrick Harris as Nightwing, and John DiMaggio as the Joker.

The film explores themes of redemption, revenge, and morality, earning praise from critics for its compelling plot, animation, and storytelling. Regarded as one of the best in the DC Universe Animated Original Movies series, it achieved commercial success, grossing over $12 million in home video sales.
